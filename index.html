Events

Our goal at Botnet JUET is to educate students about the requirements and demands of global security. To educate the community at JUET Guna about cybersecurity and other cutting-edge technology, our team hosts a number of activities. The following is a list of a few.

Project Neer (Mud pot filter project)

Our vision is to provide clean drinking water and to uplift the potter community. We create employement opportunities for the lower sections of society and thus lend a helping hand to make them self-reliant.
Motivation We plan at targeting people facing problems due to handpumps and borewells. Prayagraj being located on the shores of two major rivers faces the problem of inclusion of sand and silt in drinking water which can be resolved using ceramic mud pot filters.
Objectives Primary objective is to increase the access to safe, and sustainable water to people in cost effective way through cheaper water purifier according to needs of Prayagraj city. We encourage the community of potters and help them in connecting with the market for their upliftment. Alongside , the project majorly reduces the use of plastic in water purifiers and to enhance the usage of clay, mud and other natural degradable resources.

Project Sahyog

Our vision is to enhance and strengthen the already available initiatives by government and various other non government bodies by bringing synergy among them and training the target masses for making the best of opportunities.
Motivation Despite the efforts by various government bodies for the betterment of society, the true goal is still far from reach. In the fast evolving digital era, a particular segment of society is left behind. Lack of awareness and information is key observed reason for holding back the underprivileged segment.
Goal Conducting various training camps to train rural people (primarily youths) to make use of various crucial digital services. Organising awareness campaigns about various crucial livelihood schemes and how to access them. Channeling and supporting various other government bodies by providing R&D.

Project Moonj

Our most recent venture in collaboration with the initiative by MHRD and state government of Uttar Pradesh under 'One District One Product Scheme'. The project aims at empowering the indigenious Moonj based handicraft products which have been facing existential challenge over the years.
Motivation The Naini area of Allahabad has been recognised widely for its Moonj Craft. But with time iy has been losing its charm and today it is significantly far from the time of its glory. The market offers variety of products made from the moonj like basket(daliya in Hindi), coaster stand, bags, decorative items and more. Eco-friendly moonj products have the potential to do well in the national as well as international markets.
Goal The team aspires to look for taking he plunge into variuos aspects related to supply chain, marketing, promotion and technological R&D assistance to tackle the various challenges which have been holding back this art to achieve the glory it deserves.

Project Samvesh (Paper bag Project)

To rejuvenate from rubble, to turn a liabilty into a profitable asset keeping in view the motto "Enterpreneurial Action for others", is something which stands as the backbone of our project of dreams-Samvesh, the maiden project of Enactus MNNIT Allahabad.
Vision To launch a viable alternative for polythene bags, aiming at Community Empowerment and Self-Reliance. To majorly target women, providing them with required aids, thus enhancing their skills as project workers.
What we aim for To launch a viable alternative for polythene bags aiming at Community Empowerment and Self-Reliance. The project majorly target women, providing them with required aids, thus enhancing their skills as project workers.
Final Product A sturdy eco-friendly bag made out of discarded newspapers and magazines. Strings attached to the paper bags provide a better grip and a ajor step-up from the conventional ones. Replacing syntheticglues with wheat paste to stick the bag together. Endurable and tough, the quality is unbeatable.
